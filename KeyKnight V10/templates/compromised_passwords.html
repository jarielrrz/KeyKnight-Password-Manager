The Password Rule of Thumb

Make your passwords long, at least 12 characters.

Use a mix of big and small letters, numbers, and symbols to make them tricky.

Never use the same password twice; each account needs its own unique one.

Avoid using personal stuff like your name or birthday.

Consider using a passphrase, which is a bunch of words that are easy for you to remember but hard for others to guess.
